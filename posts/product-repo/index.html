Product Repo: A Curated Collection of Product Management Resources

November 27, 2024

Product Repo is my personal, curated collection of product management articles that I find worth reading. The list is organized into categories such as product-led growth, strategy, leadership, product management fundamentals and many more.

You can find it here: https://github.com/sischreiber/product-repo/

It’s essentially a GitHub readme file in my rather sparse repo, which I update sporadically whenever I come across noteworthy articles. Product Repo is inspired by https://github.com/ProductHired/open-product-management which unfortunately hasn’t been updated in several years.
