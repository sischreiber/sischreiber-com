About

I’m a freelance product manager based in Munich, Germany. For over a decade, I’ve been helping organizations, from start-ups to established brands, transform ideas into commercial software products. I enjoy working on products end to end, from ideation all the way to launch.

I have extensive experience in growing products, building products from scratch and leading cross-functional teams at organizations such as waipu.tv, jameda, Miele X, ProSiebenSat1, AutoScout24, Telefónica and the Bundesliga.

I believe in product-led growth. It’s about making the product the center of the buying journey and the broader customer experience. This evidence-based approach to product development is built on a close collaboration with engineering, UX/UI, marketing, finance and all other stakeholders involved to ensure the product aligns with the needs of the market in the best possible way.

Ping me if you’d like to hear more about it.
